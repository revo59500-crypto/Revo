PULSE. RETURN

WE GOT TIRED OF BORING SODA.

In 2026, the beverage aisle looked exactly like it did in 1996. Same sticky syrups, same corporate brands, same sluggish feeling after you drank one.

Pulse was built in a garage by three web developers who needed a clean, sustained energy source to code through the night. We treated hydration like software: we debugged the sugar crashes, optimized the flavor profiles, and deployed the final build.

V 1.0

The first batch of Cyber Lime is formulated. We gave it away at local hackathons. It disappeared in minutes.

V 2.0

We switched to 100% sustainable aluminum and finalized the cryo-extraction process, achieving the signature "Snap."
