Welcome to BrightSmile

Since 2010, BrightSmile Dental Clinic has been providing comprehensive dental care for families in our community. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure your visits are efficient, safe, and comfortable.

Our philosophy is simple: treat every patient like family. We listen to your concerns, explain your treatment options clearly, and focus heavily on preventative care to keep your natural teeth healthy for life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes! We are always excited to welcome new patients to our dental family. You can book your first appointment using the form below.

Do you take my insurance?

We accept most major PPO dental insurance plans. Our front desk team will happily verify your benefits before your appointment so there are no surprise costs.

What if I have a dental emergency after hours?

If you are a patient of record experiencing a true dental emergency, call our main office number and follow the prompts to be connected to our on-call dentist.
